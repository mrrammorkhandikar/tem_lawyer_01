Consultation FAQ

Common questions about our consultation process.

Please bring any relevant documents related to your legal matter, including contracts, correspondence, court documents, or other materials. For video consultations, have digital copies ready to share. We'll also send you a preparation checklist after booking.

Consultation fees are generally non-refundable once the session is completed. However, if you need to reschedule with at least 24 hours notice, there's no additional charge. The consultation fee is often credited toward your retainer if you decide to engage our services.

Consultation duration varies by type: phone consultations are 30 minutes, video conferences are 45 minutes, and in-person meetings are 60 minutes. We respect your time and ensure you receive comprehensive attention within the scheduled timeframe.

After your consultation, you'll receive a summary email within 24 hours outlining our discussion, recommended next steps, and a proposed engagement letter if you decide to proceed. There's no obligation to hire us, and we're happy to provide referrals if we're not the right fit.
